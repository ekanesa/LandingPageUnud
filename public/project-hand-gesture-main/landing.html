What is This Project?

This project introduces a revolutionary approach to human-computer interaction, leveraging advanced real-time hand gesture recognition. By utilizing MediaPipe's robust hand tracking capabilities, we've developed a system that allows users to trigger actions with simple, intuitive hand movements, eliminating the need for physical contact.

It's designed to explore the potential of hygienic, intuitive, and futuristic control mechanisms for various applications, showcasing the power of computer vision in creating more natural user experiences.

How It Works

1. Initiate Camera

Your device's camera activates, preparing to detect your hands.

2. Detect Open Hand

The system intelligently identifies a stable open hand gesture.

3. Countdown for Stability

A brief countdown ensures the gesture is intentional and stable.

4. Launch Action

Upon countdown completion, your desired "product" is launched!
